Idea 1: Canva Workflow

This page simulates the hybrid workflow: create the polished artwork in Canva, export it, then merge live student data inside this app for preview and print.

All Ideas Admin

How It Works

This option is best when your team wants designer-quality visuals without building a full design engine first.

1. Design in CanvaMake the card front in Canva with logo, textures, shapes, and branding.
2. Export ArtworkExport as transparent PNG or full background artwork and upload it to this system.
3. Merge Live DataWe place photo, name, serial, dates, and role in controlled print-safe positions.
4. Print A4Use the app for layout count, margins, preview, and final print output.

Strong fit if principal, admin staff, or designers want to visually edit card art without touching code.
